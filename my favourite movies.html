The Movies I Like

In the hustle of daily life, movies serve as a therapeutic escape for me. Whether nestled in a cinema seat or lounging at home, the captivating stories and characters offer a respite from reality's demands. For me, it's not just about entertainment; it's a chance to live vicariously through the diverse narratives, finding solace, joy, and inspiration in the temporary worlds crafted on the screen.

Here Are The Top Three Movies In My List

GOODFELLAS



"Goodfellas" is a classic crime film directed by Martin Scorsese, depicting the rise and fall of Henry Hill within the Italian-American mob. Filled with intense performances, dynamic storytelling, and gritty realism, the movie explores the allure and consequences of a life in organized crime.


My rating for the movie is 9.0/10


INTERSTELLAR



"Interstellar," directed by Christopher Nolan, is a sci-fi epic that follows a group of astronauts on a perilous journey through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The film intricately weaves complex themes of love, time dilation, and the survival of humanity, offering a visually stunning and emotionally gripping cinematic experience.


My rating for the movie is 9.5/10


INTO THE WILD



"Into the Wild," directed by Sean Penn, chronicles the true story of Christopher McCandless, a young man who abandons societal norms to embark on a solo journey into the Alaskan wilderness. The film explores themes of self-discovery, freedom, and the consequences of seeking an uncharted path in pursuit of a meaningful existence.


My rating for the movie is 9.2/10
